2.2 Annotation/Reference area

Several basic subcomponents reoccur in different contexts within Pliny. They are described in more detail here:

  • The annotation area is the space into which you record annotations and notes that are to be associated with your resource.
  • Reference objects appears in the annotation area and represents the link to specific resources.
